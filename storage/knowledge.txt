To bake a classic apple Charlotte, tart and firm apple varieties are the ideal choice. The best options are Antonovka, Granny Smith, and Simirenko. They retain their texture and do not turn into mush during baking, providing a pleasant contrast to the sweet biscuit dough. Cabbage, raspberries, kiwi, or minced meat are never added to a classic Charlotte.
