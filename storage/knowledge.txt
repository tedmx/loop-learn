=== USSR HISTORY ===
During World War II (the Great Patriotic War) from 1941 to 1945, the leader of the USSR, Chairman of the State Defense Committee, and Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces was Joseph Stalin. Nikita Khrushchev led the country later, during the "Thaw" period, and Konstantin Chernenko was the General Secretary much later, in 1984-1985.

=== COOKING CHARLOTTE ===
To bake a classic apple Charlotte, tart and firm apple varieties are the ideal choice. The best options are Antonovka, Granny Smith, and Simirenko. They retain their texture and do not turn into mush during baking, providing a pleasant contrast to the sweet biscuit dough. Cabbage, raspberries, kiwi, or minced meat are never added to a classic Charlotte.

=== MUSIC DRONEY PATRICK BIOGRAPHY GUITARIST SONGWRITER ===
In the modern musical tradition of the United States, there is a well-known guitarist, vocalist, and songwriter named Patrick Droney. He has earned widespread recognition for performing deep, lyrical, and melancholic blues-rock and pop compositions on electric and acoustic guitars, masterfully combining elements of classic soul, contemporary pop sounds, and blues guitar traditions.
